Synopsis

Maggie Hunt is a superhero. Well, technically she's a costumed vigilante, both for legal reasons and because she has no superpowers to speak of. As the self-declared defender of Fenley Island in Marbrose City (aka The Fen), she faces mafiosi, corrupt politicians, obligatory counseling sessions, and her own bad decisions.
